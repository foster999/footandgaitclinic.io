Coronavirus Update

Following further government updates, we will open our practice on Monday 6th April.

We have made treatments available virtually where we can and we will be offering the following patients appointments on site at The Foot and Gait Clinic:

  • Emergency Treatment for Patients
  • Treatment for At Risk Patients
  • Treatment for Key Workers
  • Treatment for Patients in Pain

Attending an appointment, on site at the Foot and Gait Clinic

When attending an appointment, we would ask you to please wait in our car park at the back of the practice. This will avoid the use of our waiting room. We will bring you into the clinic at your appointment time.

Our practice is purpose built, spacious and easy to clean. We are spacing our appointments to ensure social distancing and to undertake vigorous cleaning regimes before and after each patient.

We have provided a hand washing station as you come into the practice and ask you to please wash your hands on arrival. We are also using contactless payments to minimise contact further.

We cannot treat patients who either are currently unwell with COVID-19 or have been in contact with someone who has. As per government guidelines, these people should isolate for up to 14 days.

Virtual Appointments

We have pioneered a virtual appointment system in our practice. We are pleased to report its success! If you would like a Telehealth appointment with our Physiotherapist, James, Rehabilitation Therapist, Fiona or with one of our Musculoskeletal Podiatrists, Marian or Alastair, please ring for an appointment.

How Telehealth works:

When you make a Telehealth appointment a confirmation email is sent to you. At the bottom of this email, there is a link. On the day and time of your appointment, please click this link, which will start the virtual appointment. It is a video and audio system that is secure and linked to our clinical notes. We can talk to you directly via the internet, wherever you are. Using this system, we have treated patients from Taunton, Edinburgh, Australia and New Zealand! Please pay before the appointment using BACs.

If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hestitate to ring, there is always someone at the end of the phone, 9am-5pm Monday-Friday.

Take care all, keep well and safe,

Welcome to The Foot & Gait Clinic

Our foot and gait clinic started 30 years ago in 1987 in Taunton. We have gradually expanded over the years, until finally we moved to our purpose built premises here at 31 Bridge Street.

We have always worked in joint practices, feeling that the best treatments for our patients come from sharing knowledge and skills from practitioners that are experts in their fields.

Over the years we have built up wonderful working relationships with Osteopaths, Physiotherapists, Chiropractors and Sport's Massage Therapists.

As well as the work carried out in our practice, we also run external clinics including the following GP practices:

  • Bishops Lydeard Health Centre
  • Comeytrowe Medical Centre
  • Warwick House Medical Centre
